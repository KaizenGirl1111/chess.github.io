Chess in mordern world

Chess is a brainstorming game that lost its lustre in the era of pubg, thanks to Chess promoters like Samay Raina and series like The Queens Gambit it has bounced back and reclaimed its position as a significant game in todays time.

Chess has 3 parts:

  1. the beginning game
  2. the middle game
  3. the end game.

The pieces of chess are as follows-

  1. Pawn- 8 of 1 point each
  2. Knight- 2 of 3 points each
  3. Bishop- 2 of 3 points each
  4. Rook- 2 of 5 points each
  5. Queen- 1 of 9 points
